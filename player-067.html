Kang Sae-byeok (Player 067)

Biography

Kang Sae-byeok is a North Korean defector living in South Korea. She enters the games to win the prize money to reunite her family, specifically to buy her brother out of an orphanage and bring her mother out of North Korea. She is stoic, distrustful, and highly observant.

Role in the Games

Sae-byeok proves to be one of the most capable players, excelling in physical challenges and strategic thinking. She forms a tentative alliance with Gi-hun and Sang-woo. Her backstory reveals the harsh realities faced by defectors, adding emotional depth to her struggle for survival.

Key Traits

  • Resilience: Survived extreme hardship crossing the border from North Korea.
  • Skills: Expert pickpocket and street fighter.
  • Fate: Tragically stabbed by Sang-woo in the final stages; dies in Gi-hun's arms just before winning.
