Oh Il-nam (Player 001)

Biography

Oh Il-nam appears as an elderly man suffering from a brain tumour. Initially perceived as frail and confused, he forms a close bond with Seong Gi-hun (Player 456). Despite his age, he displays surprising resilience during the games.

Role in the Games

Il-nam participates in all games alongside the other contestants. His knowledge of the games' mechanics often helps his team survive. In a shocking twist, it is revealed that he is actually the host and creator of the Squid Game competition, having joined as a player to experience the thrill one last time before his death.

Key Traits

  • Intelligence: Extremely high; mastermind behind the entire operation.
  • Motivation: Boredom with life and a desire to feel excitement again.
  • Fate: Passes away peacefully after watching the final game with Gi-hun.
