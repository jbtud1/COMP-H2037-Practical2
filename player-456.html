Seong Gi-hun (Player 456)

Biography

Seong Gi-hun is the protagonist of the series. A divorced father struggling with gambling addiction and debt, he is invited to play the games to solve his financial woes and gain custody of his daughter. Despite his flaws, he possesses a strong moral compass and empathy for others.

Role in the Games

Gi-hun survives all six games, often relying on luck, the help of allies, and his own moments of brilliance. Unlike many other players, he frequently attempts to protect weaker contestants. He ultimately wins the competition but is left traumatised by the loss of his friends.

Key Traits

  • Empathy: Frequently risks his life to help others, contrasting with the selfish nature of the game.
  • Growth: Transforms from a selfish gambler to a selfless individual who rejects the prize money's corrupting influence.
  • Fate: The sole winner of the 33rd Squid Game; decides not to board a plane to see his daughter, choosing instead to stop the games.
